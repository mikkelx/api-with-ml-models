About

Our application utilizes ensemble learning techniques, specifically bagging and stacking, to accurately classify clothing items from the Fashion-MNIST dataset. By combining multiple classifiers and their results, we achieve precise and reliable clothing recognition. Experience the power of ensemble learning in fashion classification with our innovative application.

Our application accepts color images as input, which are then automatically transformed into black and white and applied with a negative effect. This preprocessing step is specifically designed to align the input data with the training set, which consists of black and white images from the Fashion-MNIST dataset used to train our convolutional neural network models. By adapting the input images to match the training data, our application ensures optimal performance and accurate classification results. Experience the seamless integration of color image processing with our advanced application.
