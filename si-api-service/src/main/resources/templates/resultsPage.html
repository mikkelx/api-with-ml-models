About

Our application utilizes ensemble learning techniques, specifically bagging and stacking, to accurately classify clothing items from the Fashion-MNIST dataset. By combining multiple classifiers and their results, we achieve precise and reliable clothing recognition. Experience the power of ensemble learning in fashion classification with our innovative application.

Objects that can be used for classification based on Fashion-MNIST:

  1. T-shirts: Images depicting various patterns and styles of t-shirts, including both short and long sleeves.
  2. Pants: Pictures of different types of pants such as jeans, trousers, and sportswear.
  3. Sweaters: Images of sweaters with different styles, patterns, and sleeve lengths.
  4. Dresses: Photos of dresses in different lengths, patterns, and styles, including cocktail dresses, evening gowns, and summer dresses.
  5. Jackets: Pictures of various types of jackets, such as winter jackets, denim jackets, leather jackets, and down jackets.
  6. Sandals: Images of different types of sandals, including flats and high heels.
  7. Shirts: Pictures of different styles of shirts, including formal shirts, blouses, and tunics.
  8. Sneakers: Images of sports shoes, such as sneakers, running shoes, and basketball shoes.
  9. Bags: Photos of various types of bags, including shoulder bags, backpacks, and laptop bags.
  10. High-heeled shoes: Images of different types of high-heeled shoes, such as pumps, court shoes, and high-heeled sandals.

Our application accepts color images as input, which are then automatically transformed into black and white and applied with a negative effect. This preprocessing step is specifically designed to align the input data with the training set, which consists of black and white images from the Fashion-MNIST dataset used to train our convolutional neural network models. By adapting the input images to match the training data, our application ensures optimal performance and accurate classification results. Experience the seamless integration of color image processing with our advanced application.
